Announcements

    • There is lab this week. Bring a laptop. You can borrow one for the semester from the library if you don't have your own. See Piazza for details.
    • Sign up for one of our connector courses!
    • Create your computing account by going to datahub.berkeley.edu and logging in with your @berkeley.edu account. Try starting your server. It takes about a minute. If you have problems, let us know by coming to lab and asking questions or posting on Piazza.
    • You must be enrolled in a lab section in order to be enrolled in the course. If you're on the waitlist, the reason you are waitlisted is because your lab is full.
